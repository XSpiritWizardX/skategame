Street Push

Kick, jump, and grind past the city junk.

Score: 0 Best: 0 Speed: 1.0x

Ready to ride?

Press Space to jump, Shift to kick (short boost), and press R to restart.

Controls
  • Space: jump / ollie
  • Shift: kick for a burst
  • Arrow Down: crouch to roll under bars
  • R: restart
Goal

Chain clean landings and boosts to build the score multiplier. Watch for cones, bins, and low bars.
